Transportation

📱

Uber — Recommended

Available throughout Isla Verde & San Juan metro. Always use at night. Not available in rural mountain areas (Orocovis, Toro Verde).

🚕

Local taxis

White taxis at hotels & airports. Negotiate fare before getting in — meters not always used. Ask hotel to call a reliable one.

Safety Tips

!

Nothing in the car

Never leave bags, electronics, cash, or passports visible in a parked vehicle — even for a minute.

!

Beaches after dark

Avoid the beach alone after midnight. Stick to well-lit areas near the hotels.

!

Don't drink & drive

DUI laws are strictly enforced. Limit 0.08%. Always take Uber after a night out.

☀️

Intense sun

Use SPF 50+ sunscreen, reapply every 2 hours. Avoid prolonged exposure 10 AM–3 PM.

💧

Stay hydrated

Heat and humidity dehydrate you fast — drink water constantly, especially at the beach or drinking alcohol.

⚡

Power outages

Power outages are common in PR. Keep your phone charged. Contact us if you need help.
